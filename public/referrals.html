How the Referral Program Works

3-Level Commission Structure

Example Scenario:

Let's say you refer User B who deposits 1,000 USDT:

  • You earn: 50 USDT (5% of 1,000 USDT)

Then User B refers User C who deposits 1,000 USDT:

  • User B earns: 50 USDT (5% of 1,000 USDT)
  • You earn: 20 USDT (2% of 1,000 USDT)

Finally, User C refers User D who deposits 1,000 USDT:

  • User C earns: 50 USDT (5% of 1,000 USDT)
  • User B earns: 20 USDT (2% of 1,000 USDT)
  • You earn: 10 USDT (1% of 1,000 USDT)

Tips for Successful Referrals

  • Share your link with people who are genuinely interested in investing
  • Explain the benefits of the platform and your own positive experience
  • Use social media, forums, and investment communities to find potential referrals
  • Follow up with your referrals to ensure they complete the registration and deposit process
  • Create content like blog posts, videos or reviews to attract more referrals
